How to Play
👥
Two Teams
Take turns describing words to your team. The other team watches for cheating!
🚫
Taboo Words
Never say the 5 forbidden words on the card! Get caught = point for the other team.
⏭️
3 Skips
Each round you get 3 skips. Use them wisely — skipped cards don't count against you!
🏆
Winning
Play 3 rounds each. Team with the most points wins!
